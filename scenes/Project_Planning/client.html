2.1.1 Software Distribution Models:

VSP - Virtual Service Provisioning Platform

2.1.2 Key aspects:


What benefits will you get from your Billing Platform?
The Billing Platform offers you a platform to automate all your billing requirements and functions, by fully integrating to your existing financial system. The platform allows you to not only manage your customers, invoicing, payments and financial reports, it also provides you with full management and control of service offerings.



What is your VSP Platform?

The Virtual Service Provider Platform offers you a platform to automate service provisioning. The platform allows you to not only manage your valued customers, but also the services and poduct you make available to them.



2.1.3 Numista Deliverables

Invoice & Pro-Forma Automation:
Automatically create once off and Pro-Forma invoices for new services or products ordered

Aunuity Billing Management & Automation:
Automatically create and bill annuity items and services, never missing billing.

Secure Payment Collection & Automated Allocation:
Billing platform seamlessly integrates into many different payment processors to allow for automated payment collection via Credit Card, Debit Order or instant EFT.
The payment option is also made available to the client through the ClientZone portal.

Quote & Service Order Management:
Use billing software to quote your customers and generate digitally signed new service orders forms.
The billing platform can also notify you of upcoming contract renewals, or report on unaccepted quotations.

Reporting:
The billing platform has a reporting module to generate financial and sales reports at the click of a button.

2.4 Solution Design

This section will describe the software systems, system users
and integrations forming part of the Nowtech software project.


The Business Intelligence Platform

Purpose:
Billing platform providing financial automation, ordering, invoicing and reporting.
Functions:
Invoice and pro-forma automation
Annuity billing management and automation
Secure payment collection and automated allocation
Quote and service order management
Financial reporting
Users:
Organization staff members only, the following permissions can be applied:
Management user permissions
Financial user permissions
Admin user permissions
Sales user permissions
Support user permissions



VSP – The Virtual Service Provisioning Portal

Purpose:
Service provisioning and ordering system, and customer management portal
Functions:
Management of users, resellers and customers
Management of voice rates applied to resellers and customers
Can allow or deny VPS services
Management of VPS price plans including packages and add-on's
Can view and process connectivity orders, and do feasibility checks
Requests for number porting can be processed from the system
Telephone numbers can be provisioned to customers from VSP
Call records can be viewed and downloaded
Users:
The users that will make use of the system:
Organization: Full access and permissions allowed
Customers: No access, accounts and services are managed by Nowtech and/or the respective branches and business partners
